About Karo Pitch

Built for Founders
Across Bharat

Thousands of incredible founders are building real businesses from Patna to Surat, from Coimbatore to Bhopal — yet they struggle to access the investors, networks, and visibility they deserve.

Karo Pitch changes that. We connect India's most ambitious early-stage founders directly with investors through curated pitch events and a powerful discovery platform — no gatekeepers, no geography barriers.

"India's next unicorn isn't just being built in Bangalore or Mumbai. It's being built everywhere. Karo Pitch makes sure every founder gets a fair shot."

How It Works

From Application
to Funding

A clear, structured process designed to give every founder the best chance of finding the right investor.

01
📋
Apply with Your Pitch Deck
Fill out a short application form and share your pitch deck or a 2-minute video explaining your business.
02
🔍
Get Shortlisted
Our team at KaroStartup reviews every application. Selected founders receive an invite for the live pitch event.
03
🎤
Pitch Live to Investors
Present your startup in a structured 10-minute pitch session in front of a curated panel of investors.
04
🚀
Raise Funding & Scale
Receive term sheets, investor introductions, and ongoing support to raise capital and accelerate growth.
